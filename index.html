SHEEZ LANLA HOSPITAL Hospital is well equipped to provide a gamut of medical services including OPD, Inward care, mobile services, dental services and more manned by highly trained professionals

SHEEZ LANKA Hospital (SLH) in Colombo is the largest private hospital in Sri Lanka and the last referral centre in the country with 1000 beds. It is a training center for undergraduates and postgraduate trainees of the Faculty of Medicine. The Colombo Nursing Training School, PBS and the schools of Radiation, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy and Occupational Therapy are also affiliated to our hospital.
